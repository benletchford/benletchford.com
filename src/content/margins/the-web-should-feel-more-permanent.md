---
title: "The web should feel more permanent"
date: "2026-06-22"
section: "Web"
description: "A short argument for pages that can be read, saved, and returned to without ceremony."
tags:
  - web
---

The web does not need to feel old to feel permanent. It needs pages with stable
addresses, ordinary links, readable text, and no assumption that the visitor
has arrived through a campaign funnel.

Permanence is mostly restraint. Fewer overlays. Fewer dependencies. Fewer
reasons for a page to forget what it was.
