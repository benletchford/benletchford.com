The interesting transition is not from human work to machine work. It is from
implicit judgement to explicit boundaries.

AI systems make it cheap to produce plausible output. That does not remove the
need for taste, responsibility, or intent. It moves those things upstream, into
the design of the system and the constraints around it.

The better tool is not the one that pretends the user has disappeared. The
better tool makes the user's judgement more precise. It exposes the point
where a decision matters, then gets out of the way.
